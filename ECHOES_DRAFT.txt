POCKET PARAMOUR — ECHO SYSTEM DRAFT
====================================
Mark any line you want to change, cut, or soften. Then tell me "looks good"
or "rewrite these" and I'll wire the approved set into js/world-echoes.js.

How this will work in-game:
- When a world-state flag flips (a turning point is chosen, a chapter ends),
  every OTHER character stores a "pending echo line."
- The next time the player opens that character, ONE echo line surfaces —
  either as an extra dialogue beat, or an italic thought before their intro.
- Each echo fires ONCE per character per trigger. Never spammed.
